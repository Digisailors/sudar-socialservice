Who We Are?

At Sudar Social Services, we believe in creating a better world through compassion, action, and community support. Founded with a mission to uplift the underprivileged, we work towards empowering individuals and communities through education, healthcare, environmental initiatives, and social welfare programs. Our journey began with a simple vision—to bring hope and opportunities to those in need. Over time, we have grown into a dedicated team of volunteers, changemakers, and supporters who work tirelessly to make a meaningful impact.
